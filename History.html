history

The action begins shortly after the death of the second wife Kratos and mother Atreus Fay, whose last will was dispel her remains from the highest peak of all nine worlds. Before the journey, Kratos meets a stranger with divine powers whose body is completely covered tattoos. He demands answers from Kratos and taunts his past, which leads to a conflict that turned into fierce battle. Defeating the stranger, Kratos along with Atreus go on a journey.
